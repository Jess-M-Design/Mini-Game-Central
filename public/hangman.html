Let's Play Hangman

This is a simple game of Hangman. You've played Hangman before, right? Well, in this game, the player chooses letters to guess the hidden word. Simply click a letter or type on your keyboard to start. If you have already used a letter, it won't be repeated so don't worry about miss clicking the same letter twice, but if you click the wrong letter, you will lose a life. Good Luck!
